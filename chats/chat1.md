Context
Build a low-fidelity wireframe for a landing page promoting Design Asylum Studio's productized mobile app design service. The studio is a Pune-based brand-to-product design firm with 10+ years of experience, selling B2C mobile app design delivered in 6 weeks. Traffic comes from paid Meta and Google ads.
This is a structural wireframe only. No visual design, no color, no decoration, no animation, no interactions.
Wireframe Rules

Greyscale only. White background (#FFFFFF), light gray surfaces (#F2F2F2 or #EEEEEE), near-black text (#0A0A0A), medium gray for placeholder labels (#999999).
No colors, gradients, shadows, blurs, or accent hues.
No hover states, no reveal animations, no scroll triggers, no transitions, no carousel auto-advance. Static elements only.
No real imagery. Use plain gray rectangles with a centered label like "Image", "App Screen", "Logo", or "Avatar".
One typeface. Use system sans-serif or Inter. Single font family, two or three weights at most (regular, semibold, bold).
Boxes, lines, and text. Borders are 1px solid light gray where needed. Corner radius minimal (0 to 8px). No icons unless drawn as simple line shapes.
Layout is the message. Spacing, alignment, and hierarchy should communicate structure without decoration.
Fully responsive. Single file, mobile-first, breakpoints at 360, 768, 1024, 1440.
