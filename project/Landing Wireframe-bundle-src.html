Questions

What happens after I click Build My App?

You'll book a 30-minute intake call with our design lead. We send a one-page scoping doc within 48 hours, plus a fixed-fee contract. Work starts as soon as the contract is signed and the first invoice clears.

What if my app needs more than 3 core features?

The 3-feature scope is the productized version. Beyond that we quote a custom engagement — same process, same deliverables, sized to your scope. Most extensions add one to three weeks per additional feature set.

Do you build the app or just design it?

Design only. You receive a clickable prototype, a Figma file with spec'd components, and an asset library that's ready for engineering. We can introduce you to vetted dev partners we've shipped with, or work alongside your in-house team.

Who owns the source files?

You do, on final payment. Full IP transfer covers the Figma file, exported assets, prototype, and any source illustrations we delivered. We retain the right to show the work in our portfolio unless you ask us not to.

What if we miss the 6-week window?

Delays caused by us are on us — we extend at no extra charge. Delays caused by feedback or scope drift on your side are quoted as additional weeks at our pro-rated rate. Either way, the price and scope you signed are locked.

Can I see the contract before paying?

Yes — the scoping doc and contract are both shared before the first invoice. Standard terms, no surprises, lawyer-reviewable. We don't take payment until you've signed.

Do you work with non-Indian clients?

Yes — roughly 40% of our work is international. We invoice in USD or EUR, accept card or wire, and run sprints across timezones (we overlap with EU mornings and US afternoons).

Who runs the test?

The client. Testing is run via usertesting.com (or a comparable platform) at the client's cost. We write the test script and define what counts as a successful completion; you commission the sessions.

Who recruits the testers?

The client, through usertesting.com or any recruit panel that matches the target audience profile. We help define the screener — age, region, behavior, prior product use — but the booking sits on your side.

What counts as "the core flow"?

A core feature is anything reachable directly from the tab bar or hamburger menu. During scope definition we co-author the epics, stories, acceptance criteria and exit criteria for that feature. Each story = one screen. Use cases and error cases beyond what the user story specifies aren't included unless added to scope.

What is the time bound on the redesign?

The free redesign window is capped at 2 additional weeks beyond the original delivery date. If we still haven't hit the bar, we move into a custom engagement and quote the remaining work transparently — never silently extend the meter.
